👤 ABOUT ME

I am a Staff Scientist at the National Institute of Allergy and Infectious Diseases (NIAID), NIH, working in the Epithelial Therapeutics Unit. My research integrates microbiome and metabolomics data to explore skin biology, with a focus on identifying biomarkers and mechanisms relevant to atopic dermatitis and related conditions. I apply a systems biology approach to study host-microbe-environment interactions and translate findings into potential therapeutic strategies. 🔬 My Key Areas of Expertise:

🧫🦠 Skin microbiome & metabolome integration
🖥️🧬 Computational analysis of 16S, metagenomics & metatranscriptomics data
💉🧪 LC-MS/GC-MS metabolomics workflows & pathway analysis
🔗📊 Multi-omics integration using statistical & machine learning methods
🌫️🧴 Environmental pollutant exposure effects on skin health

With a strong background in microbiome research across the gut, oral cavity, and skin, and experience at institutions including NTU Singapore, The Ohio State University, and Université d’Auvergne, I bring a broad, multi-disciplinary perspective to precision medicine research. Ask me about microbiome, metabolomics, systems biology, or multi-omics data integration.
